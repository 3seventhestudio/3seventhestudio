User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 2026

If you would like to request deletion of your data associated with our application, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data in one of the following ways:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to: your@email.com

Include the following information:

  • Your Instagram username
  • Your Facebook Page name (if applicable)
  • A brief description of your deletion request

Option 2: Remove the App from Facebook/Instagram

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Select our application
  4. Click "Remove"

Once removed, all associated data will be deleted from our systems within 7 business days.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Once a deletion request is received, data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.
